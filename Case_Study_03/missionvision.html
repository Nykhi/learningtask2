Mission and Vision

In commemorating our 69th founding year, we look back on our journey as a Lasallian community-- we have celebrated so many milestones, triumphs and overcame challenges together. The past year has brought on unprecedented difficulties and uncertainties, and has changed our University in so many ways; but, together as one USLS community, we have kept on with our Lasallian education Mission with fervent Faith, Zeal for Service and Communion in Mission As we move forward into the post pandemic normal, we will continue to live out the Miracle of Touching Hearts. We affirm our steadfast commitment to quality Lasallian education, expressing our aspirations in our new University of St. La Salle Vision-Mission and Core Values statements.

Vision

University of St. La Salle is a premier, responsive learning community, advancing human and Christian education, through research, innovation, and social engagement in Asia and the Pacific, in the service of humanity.

Mission

True to our Catholic tradition and inspired by St. John Baptist de La Salle, University of St. La Salle forms persons of integrity and excellence, committed to the shared mission of caring for peoples and the Earth.
